What is Make a Mark?

Make a Mark is a company that offers you a wide range of bookmarks. From watercolour bookmarks to vintage coffee bookmarks, we sell every type of bookmark. This company has been started by two school-going boys- Vihaan Upganlawar and Naman Lakhwani. It is now selling upto 40 different varieties of bookmarks and also Fridge Magnets. It has a vivid range of Custom Bookmarks and also is going to launch a series of Magnetic Bookmarks. It has a range of wildlife bookmarks and also is going to launch a FootBallers Bookmark collection. It uses only the best quality paper for its works and sells many Handmade bookmarks for affordable prices.
